Disable WiFi for Best GPS Accuracy

When WiFi is on, your iPhone may use nearby network signals for positioning instead of GPS satellites — reducing accuracy from ±4m to ±20–50m. On a 7.5ft survey grid, that's the difference between a precise heatmap and noise.

1. Open iPhone Settings
2. Tap WiFi
3. Toggle OFF
✓ Your cellular connection handles weather just fine
